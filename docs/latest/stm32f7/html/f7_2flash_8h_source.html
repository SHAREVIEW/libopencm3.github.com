42  * Differences between F7 and F4:
43  * 1. icache and dcache are now combined into a unified ART cache. The CPU has
44  * its own d/i-caches, but those are unrelated to this. They are on the
45  * AXIM bus.
46  * 4. FLASH_SR_PGSERR (programming sequence error) is now FLASH_SR_ERSERR (
47  * erase sequence error).
48  * 6. There are now two watchdogs - IWDG (independent watchdog) and WWDG (
